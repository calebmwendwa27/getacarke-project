Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car for viewing?

You can book a viewing online by selecting a car and choosing a time slot that works for you.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options depending on the seller. Be sure to check the payment terms on the car’s listing.

Do you offer car delivery services?

This solely depends on your agreement with your seller

Are all cars inspected before listing?

Absolutely. Every car goes through a basic quality check before being published to ensure it meets our marketplace standards.

Can I trade in my old car?

Yes, some sellers offer trade-in options. You can filter listings that accept trade-ins or contact support for assistance
